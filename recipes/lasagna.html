Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish featuring layered sheets of pasta with various fillings, typically including a meat sauce (ragù), a béchamel sauce, and cheese like ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
